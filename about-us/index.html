# About Event

The most Fan-Tech-Stic festival you could ever expect !

SMART Maker Festival is a non-profit, family-friendly showcase of creativity, invention, and resourcefulness and a celebration of the great minds contributing in the fields of innovation and Technologies. It’s a place where people show what they are making, and share what they are learning. It is a gathering of tech enthusiasts, fascinating, curious people who enjoy learning and love sharing what they can do. From engineers to artists to scientists to crafters, SMART Maker Festival is a venue for these “makers” to show hobbies, experiments, projects. After it's grand success in USA & Canada, Smart Maker Festival was introduced in India first in 2019 and the people of Kolkata witnessed one the largest makers’ festivals in the eastern part of the country. After 2 years of being a virtual event, Smart Maker Festival is back in the offline mode to reclaim it’s glory in 2023.

SMART Maker Festival 2023 is an amalgamation of various events such as science project exhibitions, innovative project competition, several quizzes, photography, film making , food festival, art and handicrafts, panel discussion and talk sessions.
